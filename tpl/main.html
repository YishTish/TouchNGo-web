Touch-N-Go Dashboard

Click on "Users" to see Activities sorted by users. You will see on the top bar the names of all the users who have logged activities in the system. Clicking on a user's name will show that person's activity

Click on "Locations" to see Activities sorted by locations. You will see on the top bar the names of all the locations you have set in the system, and are expecting users to register them as activities. Clicking on a location's name will show who registered an activity based on this location. You will also be able to add new locations and get QR codes in that page

Timeline is a list of all activities, by dates. Who visited which location, and when
